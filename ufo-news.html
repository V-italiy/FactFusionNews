UFOs Spotted Over Oregon Skies: Pilots Report Unusual Aerial Phenomena

On the night of December 7, 2024, pilots flying near Eugene, Oregon, encountered unidentified flying objects (UFOs) exhibiting extraordinary behavior. These sightings, captured in a 44-minute air traffic control recording, described fast-moving, glowing red circular lights performing erratic maneuvers, including corkscrew patterns at various altitudes. The incident occurred during a routine flight from Denver to Eugene, as a United Airlines pilot first reported seeing multiple objects near the Willamette National Forest. Air traffic control confirmed no military activity in the area, adding to the mystery of the phenomena. Shortly afterward, a MEDEVAC pilot in the same region also observed unusual lights moving in circles about 20 miles away.
